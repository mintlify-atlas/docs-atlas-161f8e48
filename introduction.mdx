## What is fastlane?

fastlane is a powerful automation tool for iOS and Android developers that eliminates tedious tasks from your workflow. Instead of manually managing screenshots, provisioning profiles, code signing, and app store submissions, you can automate everything with a single command.

Originally created to simplify iOS deployments, fastlane has grown into the industry-standard automation tool trusted by thousands of developers and companies worldwide.

## Core tools

fastlane includes a suite of specialized tools for common mobile development tasks:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="deliver" icon="app-store-ios">
    Upload screenshots, metadata, and binaries to the App Store. Update your app listing from the command line.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="gym" icon="hammer">
    Build and package your iOS or Mac app. Create IPA or PKG files ready for distribution.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="match" icon="lock">
    Sync code signing identities and provisioning profiles across your team using Git. The recommended approach for code signing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="snapshot" icon="camera">
    Automate taking localized screenshots of your iOS app on every device.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="scan" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Run your tests and generate beautiful reports. Integrates with XCTest and xcpretty.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="supply" icon="google-play">
    Upload screenshots, metadata, and binaries to Google Play. The Android equivalent of deliver.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="screengrab" icon="mobile">
    Automate taking localized screenshots of your Android app on every device.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="pilot" icon="plane">
    Manage your TestFlight testers and builds. Upload builds and add testers from the command line.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="cert" icon="certificate">
    Automatically create and maintain iOS code signing certificates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="sigh" icon="signature">
    Create, renew, download and repair provisioning profiles.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
